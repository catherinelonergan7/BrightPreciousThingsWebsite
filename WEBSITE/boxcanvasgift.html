Box Canvas Gifts

  My Box Canvas prints are ideal for gifts. I work in many different mediums, ranging from acrylic, mixed media, watercolour and pastels. I can create prints for your specification. I created Roller Derby Girl 555 as a gift for a family member who is the captain of the B Team of Dublin Roller Derby. I also created a print of Botticelli’s Venus which is ideal as a wedding gift as Venus is the goddess of love. Prices are available by request. You can follow my Instagram page for more images of the box canvas’s.

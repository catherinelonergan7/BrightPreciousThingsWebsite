About the Artist

Dublin born artist Catherine Lonergan creates unique art pieces and water colour cards. She works under the name Bright Precious Things which comes from a quote from the Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald “all the bright, precious things fade so fast and they don’t come back.” Art can preserve these bright precious moments in life like a fly trapped in amber. By using techniques such as pressing flowers which would decay otherwise, beauty can be captured and preserved. Catherine works in various mediums such as acrylic, watercolour, pastels, and mixed media. She can accommodate requests to suit the client’s needs. Prices are available by request. You can contact her though the contact page.

About the Woodland Series

Catherine completed a series of artworks based on the four corners woodlands of Rathcoole. Collecting twigs, branches, and flowers she incorporated them into the paintings. The series was inspired by the need to preserve this woodland area which is under threat now. There have been plans to demolish the woodlands.
